SIR M VISVESVARAYA BLOCK

About the Block

Ground Floor: Main entrance and common facilities.

2nd Floor: Houses the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE).

3rd Floor: Includes the BEE Lab, PG Lab, Logic Design Lab, and Power Electronics Lab, providing practical training in core electrical and electronics concepts.

4th Floor: Home to the Department of CCE, along with the MATLAB Lab and Electric Vehicle Lab, encouraging exploration in computational engineering and sustainable technologies.

5th Floor: Features the IoT Lab, AIML Lab, Algorithms Lab, CVA Lab, and Intel AI Skills Lab, supporting advanced learning and projects in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and IoT applications.

6th Floor: Houses the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AIDS), fostering research and development in data-driven technologies and intelligent systems.

Location: Near the central library, adjacent to Aryabhata Block.
