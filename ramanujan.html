S. RAMANUJAN BLOCK

About the Block

The S. Ramanujan Block is a major building at the Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology (NMAMIT) campus, primarily serving as an administrative and academic hub. It houses several key facilities, including auditoriums, laboratories, and smart classrooms that support academic and research activities.

Ground Floor: Administrative offices and main reception area.

1st Floor: Lecture halls and seminar rooms with smart classroom facilities.

2nd Floor: Research centres and departmental offices.

3rd Floor: Advanced laboratories and project spaces for interdisciplinary work.

Facilities: Labs, Smart Classrooms, Research Centres, and Wi-Fi-enabled spaces.

Location:Adjacent to APJ Block.
